Hanni Sondermann is Programme Manager of the Interface programme. The Interfaces research programme at ML@CL focuses on interpretable, self-sustaining multi-agent AI systems. It takes a systems perspective on AI adoption as software systems are the interface between socio-technical needs and AI model capabilities.
